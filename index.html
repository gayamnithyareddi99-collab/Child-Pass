About Child Pass

Child Pass is a secure digital identity platform designed to help schools, institutions and support organizations manage child identity records through a unified digital system. The platform integrates information from multiple trusted sources to create a structured and reliable digital identity for every child.

By introducing a portable digital child passport, Child Pass enables institutions to securely access and manage verified child records across different educational environments. This approach reduces fragmented data systems, improves collaboration between organizations and ensures children receive consistent support throughout their educational journey.

Child Pass Process

Child Pass follows a structured process that enables organizations to create secure digital identities, manage verification workflows and control access through a unified and reliable platform.

Step 01

Identity Data Integration

Child Pass collects and integrates child identity and educational records from multiple trusted systems such as school databases, registration platforms, SEND documentation systems and verified institutional records. The platform consolidates fragmented data into a single unified child identity profile that serves as the foundation for secure digital identity management.

Step 02

Secure Verification & Data Standardization

Once identity information is collected, Child Pass verifies and standardizes the data through secure validation processes. This ensures that records are accurate, authorized and compliant with data protection standards such as GDPR and DPA regulations. Verified data is then structured into a consistent identity model that can be safely used across institutions and systems.

Step 03

Digital Child Passport Creation

After verification, the platform generates a secure digital Child Pass that acts as a portable identity passport for the child. The passport securely carries important educational, developmental and support information so that schools, support services and authorized stakeholders can instantly access the data required to provide the right support without delays during transitions between institutions.

Step 04

Access Governance & Platform Monitoring

Child Pass provides centralized monitoring tools that allow organizations to manage permissions, track data access and analyze platform usage across the ecosystem. Through secure dashboards and governance controls, institutions can ensure transparent data sharing, maintain compliance and improve operational efficiency while protecting sensitive child information.

Digital Child Passport

A portable digital record that follows the child across institutions.

Child Pass creates a unified digital passport that stores education, health and care information in one secure profile. This allows authorized organizations to access critical data when a child moves between schools or support services.

Unified Student Records

Centralized management of educational and developmental data.

The platform consolidates fragmented student information including learning plans, assessments and support requirements into a single accessible system that reduces administrative complexity.

Secure Identity Engine

Reliable identity verification for every student profile.

Child Pass uses a canonical identity engine that anchors a child’s digital identity and ensures all records are accurately linked to the correct profile across systems and organizations.

Institutional Data Sharing

Secure data exchange between schools and authorities.

The platform enables trusted collaboration between schools, councils and support organizations through secure access permissions and controlled data sharing mechanisms.

SEND Support Integration

Support tools for children with special educational needs.

Child Pass integrates SEND information such as Education Health Care Plans (EHCPs) and specialized learning strategies so educators can provide consistent support without delays.

Secure Data Infrastructure

Enterprise-grade protection for sensitive student data.

Built with modern security architecture, Child Pass protects sensitive educational and healthcare information while maintaining strict compliance with data protection standards.
